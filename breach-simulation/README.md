# Security Incident Report — Suspicious Account Compromise

## Executive Summary
A series of failed login attempts followed by a successful authentication from an unusual location triggered a security investigation. Indicators suggested potential unauthorized access to a privileged account.

Immediate containment actions were taken to reduce risk and prevent further compromise.

---

## Incident Overview
- Incident Type: Suspicious Authentication Activity
- Severity: High
- Detection Source: SIEM alert
- Status: Contained

---

## Investigation Scope
Review authentication logs, account activity, and network indicators to determine whether compromise occurred.

---

## Key Findings
- Multiple failed login attempts observed
- Successful login from unfamiliar IP
- Privileged actions performed shortly after login

---

## Conclusion
The activity was treated as a potential account compromise and mitigated through immediate response actions.
